Cheng-Wen Fu ( James )

Carnegie Mellon University, MS Computational Design ‘27

My work sits at the intersection of design, technology, and human–computer interaction. My background spans digital fabrication, web-based development, data visualization, and computational design, including experience building automation tools with Grasshopper to support architectural workflows. These experiences have shaped my interest in creating more integrated, responsive, and intelligent design systems.

Currently, I am focusing on the relationship between AI and spatial interaction, exploring how intelligent interfaces can shape new modes of engagement between people, data, and the built environment.
